Weekly Food Drive for the Community of Gratitude Food Bank

Every Sunday, during our regular Sunday service, we invite you to bring non-perishable food items and place them in the donation cart just inside the church entrance. Thanks to the dedication of Linda and Phil, all collected items are delivered on Tuesday to the Community of Gratitude Food Bank in Ocklawaha, ensuring support reaches local families in-need promptly.

Whether it’s a couple of canned goods from your pantry or a full grocery bag, your contribution matters. Together, we keep the cart filled and share our spirit of giving with our wider community.
