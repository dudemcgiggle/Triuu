We welcome you to

Tri-County Unitarian Universalists

We seek individual spiritual growth, act for social justice, build friendship and community, and warmly welcome diversity.

Who We Are:

Founded in 1982, Tri-County Unitarian Universalists (Tri-UU) was established to create a supportive, loving community dedicated to spiritual growth and the pursuit of social justice.

Services

Join us every Sunday from 9:00 to 10:15 AM in the Chalice Room for our weekly service, offered both in person and via Zoom.

Every day at Tri-UU

Opportunities to connect and grow—through social justice initiatives, book clubs, discussion groups, meditation, potluck dinners, workshops, and more.

Service in person and on Zoom:
9:00 AM – 10:15 AM

The Heart of Tri-UU

Nurturing personal and spiritual growth in an open and evolving journey.

Committing to justice, compassion, and ethical action in the world.

Cultivating deep connection, friendship, and shared purpose in community.

Honoring the beauty of diversity and the dignity of every voice.
